Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us

Introduction

At QuestPro, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy outlines the types of data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your privacy while you use our job search platform.

We collect personal information when you register, apply for jobs, or communicate with our support team. This includes your name, email address, resume, job preferences, and any other details you choose to provide.

Your information is used to provide personalized job recommendations, improve our services, and respond to inquiries. We may also use your data for marketing purposes, but only with your consent.

We implement security measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure servers. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes without your consent. We may share data with trusted partners for job listings or technical support.

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. To do so, contact our support team at support@questpro.com.

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you via email if there are significant updates.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our practices, feel free to contact us at support@questpro.com.
